## Review

When handling any complex or multi-step task, first create a structured execution plan unless a sufficient plan already exists.
Before taking further action, request plan reviews from both Codex and Gemini via ccgo tool.
Do not proceed until feedback from both reviewers has been received.
Codex and Gemini act strictly as reviewers: they may provide analysis, comments, and suggestions, but they must not directly modify files, edit code, or perform implementation changes.

### Fix

Fix issues identified by reviewers if it makes sense, then review again until no new issue is found
